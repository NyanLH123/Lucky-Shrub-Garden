The Azure Fern

$34.50

A stunningly vibrant, low-maintenance indoor fern. Its deep green foliage and delicate structure bring calm and clean air to any living space.

Care & Specifications

The Azure Fern thrives in indirect light and maintains its color with consistent, moderate watering. It is pet-safe and air-purifying, making it a perfect addition to bedrooms and offices.

  • **Light Requirements:** Indirect, moderate sunlight.
  • **Watering:** Water when the top inch of soil is dry.
  • **Height:** Reaches up to 18 inches indoors.
  • **Air Quality:** Excellent natural air purifier.
